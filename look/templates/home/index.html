Speed

Alpha is a team of professionals who improve the created product on a daily basis, investing the accumulated knowledge and energy. Extensive experience, technological design, high organization and optimization of production processes allow us to build quickly and deliver houses on time or earlier.

Alpha is an innovator with a rational approach. We use both classic and new technologies that meet the parameters of durability and energy efficiency that appear on the market. We implement BIM-design, digitalization of engineering systems and home management using a mobile application.
